Hi and welcome to my page

The biggest thing that makes me me is my family, I have my wife Rachel, who i have been together with for 9 years and married to for 3 years. We have two amazing children Travis who is 7 and Bella who is 1 and also a scottish Terrier Nessie.

Travis is currently in year 3. He loves rugby and swimming, which he does every weekend. Bella is my little Razmanian Devil who causes destruction wherever she goes. My walls have the artwork on then to prove it.

    Motorbikes

    The biggest hobby of mine is my motorbikes. I love everything about them and can forget to stop talking about them at times. I passed my test in July 2016 and since then i have had:

    2015 Lexmoto Ranger 125cc
    Yamaha XJ600 pre-diversion 600cc
    2008 Suzuki M800 Intruder 800cc
    2000 Suzuki Bandit 1200cc

    My wife loves riding on my bikes with me.We travel out as much as we can in the summer for days out. Every year I do the reading toy run, which is a charity event with around 500-600 bikes and pillions. We all dress up in christmas ourfits and ride through reading to a Bernardos School where we all give a gift to help children in difficult situations. Every year I attend motorbike rallys with my friends, where we get to appreciate bikes, talk about bikes and ride bikes. My current motorbike is a 2000 Suzuki Bandit 1200, which is a custom design with double the horsepower it came out of the factory with so its not for the faint at heart.
